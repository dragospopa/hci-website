Prototype Evaluation

In order to evaluate our prototypes, we have decided to embrace both a Heuristic Evaluation, as well as a user-testing based evaluation. Both of these helped us achieve our final prototype as it passed both the user's expectations and our design issues.

The following table is presenting the Heuristic Evaluation that we have focused on when we designed the sketches, as well as the survey results that we have gathered from potential users.


Heuristic Evaluation
Heuristic Problem Solution
Navigation The Login button and Sign up tab are visible after loging in The Login button should be replaced with a Logout one, while the Sign Up tab will switch to Profile tab.
Aesthetics Design The Logo on all pages is too informal for an expert platform like UDL Change the Logo to fit into a more UCL-like styling of the page
User Control and Freedom All forums appear on one page The next page button is needed
