Architecture Overview

Based on the research that the team has done, the complex solution represented by the sketch prototype required the team to consider how the architecture will look. Based on this, the team has decided that the web-based platform that the team is required to design will be supported by Drupal for front-end and all the server communication. The client has mentioned that it would be best to make use of a PostgreSQL database as it would become easier to maintain after the handover at the end of the project.
