Initial Research

At the first meeting we had with our clients they instructed us to look at both the CKAN and Network of Innovators:

  • CKAN is a tool for making open data websites, it provides feathers that allow you to manage and publish collections of data.
  • The Network of Innovators is a platform targeted at experts, it has features that allow you to search the site for topics integral to governing more effectively, such as open data, citizen engagement and prize-backed challenges.
