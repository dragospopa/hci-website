Prototyping and Research

The team started using a specially designed service for Drupal development called Acquia Dev Desktop. This service improved the productivity of the team and allowed us to create a working forum that only required work on the search and the front-end. Even so, the team observed that the only way that this service allows deployment of the projects created using it would be by purchasing a paid membership to their own cloud. This would not be acceptable as the expectations of both the development team and the client would have been to build a solution that would not require additional funds.

However, the use of the service allowed us to better understand and visualize what the team wants to build in terms of design and user experience.
