General Design - Second Iteration

Second Iteration: Here the layout changed. We put the title banner on the left side of the page, and used the newly freed up space across the time top to put in the Latest Forum Updates “box”, as well as the Log In/Out and Sign Up buttons. We made the search bar bigger and placed it directly beneath the old banner space, as well as integrating all the old menu buttons into a single dropdown menu, to save space and make it look more minimalist.

Feedback:

  • There are now too few option icons and you can’t access the menu straight away.
  • Putting the banner on the (left) side doesn’t seem professional.
  • The search bar is awkwardly placed and slightly too big.


General Design - Final Iteration

Final Iteration: We then came up with our final layout. We moved the title banner back to where it was originally, but halved its size. We also moved the search bar up to the right with the login and signup buttons, and made it much smaller. The menu buttons were then moved to the left side (where the banner had been), but there were only four there now. This saved more space and made sure there was less crammed into a tight area.

Feedback:

  • The banner is now attractively sized and placed, in the top left corner.
  • The menu is easy to access from all pages, and not too much is crammed in.
  • The search bar is easy to access from all pages.
  • There is no need for a “Latest Forum Updates” area on each page, removing it saves a lot of space.
