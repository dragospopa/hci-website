Why use sketching as a prototype?

We felt that this brings the design to life - it clarifies ambiguous requirements. This particular prototype, made through sketching, supports user involvement and feedback, as users are observers in the demonstration. We believe this prototype allows us to identify problems early, so that we can use them to explore usability issues such as placing the right button in the right pages so the user can easily navigate to and away from the home page.
